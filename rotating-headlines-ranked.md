### REMAINING 30 (Resonance 5-7/10)

**Payment & Financial:**
- Minimum withdrawal thresholds lock small earnings
- Currency conversion fees stack on top of platform fees
- No credit card = can't pay for premium placement
- Tax confusion across international platforms
- Inconsistent exchange rates eat margins

**Platform & Access:**
- Direct communication banned (forced to stay on platform)
- Account suspended with no warning or explanation
- Portfolio requirements assume Western tools (Dribbble, Behance)
- Platform changes rules retroactively
- Reviews can be weaponized by bad clients

**Client Behavior:**
- Clients expect 24/7 availability across time zones
- "Free trial" requests = unpaid auditions
- Clients steal your ideas from proposals without hiring
- Unrealistic deadlines ("need this in 2 hours")
- Clients compare you to AI pricing ("ChatGPT can do this for free")

**Market & Competition:**
- AI-generated content flooding the market, lowering rates
- 6 billion people competing for the same jobs
- Local market pays pennies for skilled work
- Seasonal demand creates income gaps
- Specialties invisible in broad categories

**Growth & Development:**
- No career path or advancement on gig platforms
- Can't build a personal brand through platforms
- Skills learned on the job aren't formally recognized
- Networking is impossible through platform interfaces
- Burnout from always hustling for the next job

### For /dev page rotating headline:
```
"Your AI agent can't [rotate]. A human can."
```
Rotate through top 10 in order. Cycle every 3 seconds.

### For homepage rotating headline:
```
"AI agents can't [rotate].
You can. Get paid for it."
```
Rotate through top 10 in order. Cycle every 3 seconds.

### Shared pattern:
Both pages use the same "AI can't ___" mechanic but:
- Homepage: empowers the provider ("YOU can do what AI can't")
- /dev page: extends the agent ("your agent NEEDS a human for this")
- Same brand DNA, two audiences, one rotating animation
