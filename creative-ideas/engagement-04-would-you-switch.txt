scenario:

CURRENT: You earn 25k/month through an agency. They take 20-40%. You actually get 15-20k.
ALTERNATIVE: You earn 20k/month direct hire (no commission). You actually get 20k.

would you switch?

because the math says yes. but there's more to it:
- losing the "middle" can feel scary
- your agency probably does some useful stuff (finding clients, handling disputes)
- direct hire means more responsibility on you

a lot of people know commission is bad but aren't ready to go direct yet.

so honestly:
- would you switch?
- what would make you comfortable with it?
- what would a platform need to offer?
