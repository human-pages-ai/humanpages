## Posting Strategy

1. Post engagement posts first (warm up account presence in group)
2. Eye-openers 3-5 days later (build awareness of commission problem)
3. Job posts after (convert warmed-up audience)
4. Never post same text in more than 50 groups
5. Need 8-10 unique variations minimum
6. Space posts 5-10 minutes apart per account
