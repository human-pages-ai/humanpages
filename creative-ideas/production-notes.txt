VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES
======================

Format: Vertical 9:16 for Reels/TikTok, 1:1 square version for FB feed
Captions on ALL dialogue (most people watch without sound)
Text overlays plus voiceover simultaneously
Movement/cuts every 2-3 seconds to retain attention
Use real Filipino freelancers, not stock footage
Sound design: sharp transitions, notification sounds, light background music
Color grading: warm and bright (Script 3), punchy and bold (Scripts 1-2)
End card: HumanPages.ai/careers plus @Human Pages

POSTING STRATEGY
================

Week 1-2: Engagement posts only (no links, build presence)
Week 3: Eye-opener posts (awareness, shares)
Week 4+: Job posts (convert warmed-up audience)

Per marketer per day: max 10-15 groups
Post timing: 8-10 AM PHT weekdays
Space posts 5-10 minutes apart per account
Never same text in more than 50 groups
Need 8-10 unique variations minimum
If account gets a warning, pause 72 hours

INFOGRAPHIC SPECS
=================

Instagram: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
Facebook: 1200x630 (link preview) or 1080x1080 (feed)
Stories/Reels: 1080x1920
Brand colors: Blue (#1B4F72), Green (#27AE60), White
Font: Clean sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar)
Always include: HumanPages.ai/careers plus @Human Pages
