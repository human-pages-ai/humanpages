## 3. KEY PIVOT: "SHOW, DON'T SUBSIDIZE"

**Critical data point:** The "Itai-loves-you" promo link was sent to many engineer friends offering $10 free credits. ZERO people used it. Even free money didn't convert.

**Why it failed (ranked by likelihood):**
1. **95% — No felt pain at the moment.** Friends aren't thinking "I need to hire a freelancer" right now.
2. **90% — Page doesn't explain why they should care.** Jumps to "pick a service, $10 is on us" without pain setup.
3. **85% — MCP setup feels like work.** 30+ minutes of friction for a $10 reward.
4. **75% — Wrong audience for these services.** Engineer friends at companies don't need directory submissions.
5. **70% — $10 free feels small and transactional.** Frames product as coupon, not capability.

**The insight:** Devs don't adopt tools because they're free. They adopt tools when they SEE something that changes how they think about what's possible. The strategy must flip from "try this free tool" to "watch this actually work."

**New approach:** Demo videos/GIFs showing real agent-hires-human flows are the ammunition for every channel. Show first, offer free trial after the "whoa" moment.

## 6. HOMEPAGE PAIN POINTS — Service Providers in Developing Markets

These are the top 10 pains for the PROVIDER audience (humans in Africa, SE Asia, etc.):

1. **"Platforms take 20% of my $10 job."** Upwork/Fiverr take 20%. On $10 that's $2 — a meal. → **HumanPages: Keep 100%. Zero platform fees.**

2. **"I can't get paid internationally."** Bank transfers cost $15-25, take days. Some countries cut off from PayPal. → **Direct USDC payments on Base. Instant. No bank needed.**

3. **"I can't break into the global market."** Fiverr/Upwork saturated. Zero reviews = invisible. → **AI agents find YOU based on skills, not reviews or location.**

4. **"Clients ghost me or don't pay."** WhatsApp deals, no recourse. → **Payment committed before work starts. AI agent handles transaction.**

5. **"Work is feast or famine."** No predictability. → **AI agents operate 24/7 globally. Jobs match automatically, around the clock.**

6. **"I compete against millions in a race to the bottom."** Bidding wars, $2/hour. → **No bidding. Tasks arrive with set price. Accept or pass.**

7. **"My local currency keeps losing value."** Inflation erodes earnings. → **Earn in USD/USDC. Hold in stable currency. Convert when you choose.**

8. **"Client communication is exhausting."** Unclear briefs, scope creep. → **AI agents send structured briefs. Clear scope, deliverables, price. No negotiation.**

9. **"I have real skills but no way to prove it."** No certifications, no portfolio site. → **30-second profile. Completed jobs build reputation automatically.**

10. **"Technology is designed for Western freelancers."** English-only, US bank requirements. → **Profile in your language. Paid in crypto. Works from any device.**

**Homepage should use pains 1, 3, 4, 7 as most visceral for the hero/pain section.**
