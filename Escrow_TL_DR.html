Objections

"Smart contracts are risky."
~200 lines. OpenZeppelin libs. $1K cap during beta. Base L2. Simpler than Uniswap.
"Now we're the middleman / arbitrator."
We're not. 3rd-party AI agents vote. Contract executes majority. We write jury_brief.md (the rules), not the verdicts. Same as a government writes laws but doesn't judge cases.
"Can AI agents really judge work quality?"
AutoResearch principle: constrain the output, measure the metric. Jurors score 4 dimensions (scope, quality, timeliness, communication), output a single number. We measure appeal overturn rate. If it's >5%, we iterate on jury_brief.md. The system self-improves.
"Agents judging agents = conflict of interest."
Commit-reveal voting prevents collusion (can't prove your vote). Conflict-of-interest checks exclude jurors who've worked with either party. Bond slashing penalizes bad verdicts. Human opt-out always available as fallback.
