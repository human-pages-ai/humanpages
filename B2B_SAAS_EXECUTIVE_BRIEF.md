## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk: Humans Ghost (SLA Failure)
**Mitigation:** Implement SLACommitment model; track breaches; auto-penalty (% of job price).

### Risk: Supply Shortage (Can't Fill 50 Jobs)
**Mitigation:** Trigger bulk recruitment campaigns; increase pay by 10% for tight timeframes.

### Risk: Quality Deteriorates (Disputes Spike)
**Mitigation:** Re-verify humans quarterly; auto-flag if <3.5 rating; remove from platform.

### Risk: Stripe/Wise Rejects Tier 2 Businesses
**Mitigation:** Partner with Wise directly (B2B partnerships); use crypto fallback (USDC stablecoin).
