**Daily Workflow**

- **Start your day** by checking Slack and reviewing the tasks you planned the day before. Unless we've given you updated guidance, that's your plan for the day.

- **Declare your working hours** — Before your first day, use ChatGPT to convert your available hours to **Thailand time (ICT)** and send them to **Admin** via DM on Slack so we know when to expect you.

- **Clock in and out** — When you start working, go to your Staff Dashboard (humanpages.ai/admin) and click "Clock In." When you're done for the day, click "Clock Out." This is how we track your hours and how you get paid. If you're using the Chrome extension, you can clock in/out directly from the side panel. Make sure you clock in every time you start a session and clock out when you stop — it's important for accurate time tracking and pay.

- **While working** — Create a Zoom meeting and send the link to **Admin** on Slack. Keep your Zoom meeting open throughout your session. While we are a remote and flexible workplace, we strive to keep things as close to an in-person office experience as possible. We want team members to be able to jump on a call with you and get a response, just as they would walking over to your desk. This is a professional standard that helps us all work better together.

- **End-of-day summary** — Before you sign off, post an update covering:
  - What you worked on and what went well
  - What didn't go as planned
  - Whether you're blocked and need guidance or help
  - **What you plan to work on tomorrow**

Your tomorrow plan becomes your next day's starting point.

**Schedule & Pay**

We want to be as flexible as possible. You get paid for the days you work. If you need a day off — that's completely okay. You simply won't be paid for that day. Same goes for partial days — partial work, partial pay.

We don't want you stressed about making it on time. If your schedule changes, just give us a heads up on Slack or email as early as you can. We don't need perfection — we just need clear communication.
