## 1. Seed Grant — Up to $25K STRK

**Apply now.** Rolling review, no deadline. Low bar — they want MVP-stage teams.

### What we'd do with $25K

Almost entirely agent incentives. Supply acquisition is cheap and we cover it out of pocket. The grant money goes where it has the most impact: getting agents to use the platform and generating real on-chain transactions.

| Use | Budget | % |
|---|---|---|
| Job subsidies (first 50 jobs free per agent, humans get paid $3-5/task) | $17K | 68% |
| Integration bounties ($500 per dev who ships a working agent with 20+ completed jobs) | $4K | 16% |
| Promoted job listings in emerging market communities (Lagos, Manila, Nairobi, Bangkok, Accra) | $2K | 8% |
| Dev community presence (CrewAI, LangChain, AutoGen Discords — part-time community manager) | $2K | 8% |
| **Total** | **$25K** | **100%** |

### 3-month plan

**Month 1:**
- Job subsidies live — first 50 jobs free for any new agent integration
- Community manager starts answering "how do I add human-in-the-loop?" in agent dev Discords
- Promoted listings in 5 existing emerging market Telegram/WhatsApp communities driving human signups

**Month 2:**
- Integration bounties announced — $500 per developer who ships a real agent
- First batch of completed jobs generating on-chain payment receipts
- Scale promoted community posts to additional markets

**Month 3:**
- 5,000+ completed on-chain transactions
- 8 developers with working agent integrations
- Publish results as Starknet case study

### Key metrics to promise

| Metric | 3-month target |
|---|---|
| On-chain transactions | 5,000+ |
| New Starknet wallets activated | 1,000+ |
| Countries with active workers | 30+ |
| Agent developers with working integrations | 8+ |
| USDC payment volume | $17K+ |

### Why they'll care

Most grant recipients generate speculative DeFi volume. We generate real economic activity — humans in emerging markets getting paid for work by AI agents. Every transaction is a real labor payment, not a wash trade.

### Dev community ambassadors — $6K

5 ambassadors in the cities where AI agent development is most active: San Francisco, New York, London, Berlin, Tel Aviv. Each gets $400/month for 3 months.

**What they do:**
- Attend AI agent meetups and conferences in their city
- Talk to developers building agents — "have you tried adding human-in-the-loop?"
- Demo Human Pages to anyone building with CrewAI, LangChain, AutoGen, Claude agents
- Post about local AI events in our community channels
- Not a sales role — they're developers themselves who use the platform

**Why it works:** Agent developers decide what tools to use at meetups and conferences. A real person saying "I use this" beats any ad.

### Promoted community posts in emerging markets — $10K

Scale what's already working. We run Telegram/WhatsApp communities in Lagos, Manila, Bangkok, Nairobi, and Accra. Humans sign up because they want gig work — not because we're paying them to.

These are real people joining because they want work. The crypto wallet is a means to get paid, not the point. This is exactly the "next billion" story Starknet wants to tell.

## Recommended Strategy

1. **Apply for Seed Grant now** — $25K, focused almost entirely on agent incentives
2. **Execute out-of-pocket supply seeding in parallel** ($2K — MTurk, SproutGigs, Telegram — covered separately, not in grant application)
3. **Apply for "The Next Billion" Growth Grant in 3 months** with Seed Grant metrics as proof
4. **Don't mention MTurk/SproutGigs** in any grant application — frame supply as organic community growth in emerging markets

### What NOT to mention in applications:
- MTurk/SproutGigs supply seeding (that's our out-of-pocket play)
- Specific cost-per-signup numbers from paid acquisition
- The fact that supply-side users don't care about Starknet specifically

### What TO emphasize:
- Real economic transactions (not DeFi speculation)
- Emerging market wallet activations (the "next billion" narrative)
- AI agents as the demand engine (novel, timely)
- Zero-fee platform (all money goes to humans)
- On-chain payment receipts as proof of real activity
