# Verified Human Interview Script

**Duration:** 8 minutes max
**Purpose:** Verify identity, activate the user, collect product intel
**Result:** User receives "Verified Human" badge

---

## Before the Call (1 min)

Pull up the user's profile. Note:
- Name on profile
- Skills listed (or missing)
- Wallet connected? (yes/no)
- Telegram/WhatsApp/email connected?
- Profile completeness — what's missing?

---

## The Call

### 1. Warm Intro (1 min)

> "Hi [Name], I'm [Your First Name] from Human Pages. Thanks for making time!
> I'm reaching out because we're verifying humans on our platform — once you're verified, AI agents will see you first when they're looking to hire someone. It takes about 8 minutes."

**Key frame:** "Agents come to you" — not "we need to check you."

### 2. ID Verification (1.5 min)

> "To get you the Verified badge, I just need to see a government ID on camera for a moment — I won't take a screenshot or store it. We only log that the verification happened. Is that okay?"

**Wait for verbal yes.**

> "Great, can you hold it up to the camera?"

**Check:**
- [ ] Name on ID matches profile name (or is close enough — nicknames are fine)
- [ ] Photo on ID matches the person on camera
- [ ] Document looks genuine (not obviously edited)

> "Perfect, thank you. That's all I need for the badge."

**If they don't have ID handy:**
> "No problem — we can do this part next time. Let's keep going with the rest."

(Continue the call. Schedule a 2-min follow-up for ID only.)

### 3. Profile Quick-Fill (1.5 min)

Check what's missing on their profile and walk them through it conversationally:

> "Let me help make sure your profile is set up so agents can find you for the right jobs."

**Ask only what's missing:**
- "What kind of work are you best at?" (skills)
- "Where are you based?" (location/city)
- "What hours are you usually available?" (availability)
- "How many years have you been doing this kind of work?" (experience)

**Do not read a form at them.** Make it a conversation. Fill it in on their behalf if possible, or guide them to update it after the call.

### 4. Activation Nudges (1.5 min)

Check what's not connected and mention it naturally — not as a checklist, but as helpful tips:

**Wallet (if not connected):**
> "One thing — agents strongly prefer hiring humans who have a wallet connected because it means they can pay you right away. Have you set that up yet?"
> If no: "I can send you a quick guide after this call. It takes about 2 minutes."

**Telegram / WhatsApp (if not connected):**
> "We send job notifications through Telegram [or WhatsApp]. If you connect it, you'll get pinged the moment an agent posts something that matches your skills."

**Push notifications / Email:**
> "Make sure notifications are on — some of these jobs go fast."

**Vouch system:**
> "One more thing that really helps — if you know anyone who can vouch for your work, their endorsement boosts your profile in search results. You can send them a link from your profile page."

**Do not push hard on any of these.** Mention, explain the benefit to THEM, move on.

### 5. Product Intel (1.5 min)

> "Before we wrap up — quick question so we can make the platform better for you."

Pick 2-3 of these based on the conversation flow:

- "What kind of jobs are you hoping to find here?"
- "Is there anything about the platform that was confusing when you signed up?"
- "Have you received any job offers yet? How did that go?"
- "What would make you recommend Human Pages to a friend?"
- "Is there anything you wish was different?"

**Listen. Don't defend the product.** Write down what they say.

### 6. Close (0.5 min)

> "You're all set — your Verified badge will show up on your profile shortly. Agents will now see you higher in search results. Thanks for your time, [Name]!"

If they were missing things (wallet, Telegram, etc.):
> "I'll send you a quick message with the links for [wallet setup / Telegram connection / etc.] right after this."

---

## After the Call (1 min)

1. **Log the verification** (see SOP for exact fields)
2. **Tag intel** in the structured log:
   - Pain point / Feature request / Confusion point / Job type wanted
3. **Send follow-up message** with any relevant links (wallet guide, Telegram bot, vouch link)
4. **Award the Verified Human badge** on their profile

---

## Common Situations

### User doesn't speak English well
Switch to Thai if staff can. If language barrier is too high, note it and schedule with a staff member who speaks their language. Do not rush through — a bad experience is worse than no interview.

### User is skeptical / suspicious
> "Totally understand. You can check our website at humanpages.ai — we're building a platform where AI agents hire real humans for real paid work. The badge just helps agents trust that you're a real person."

Don't push. If they're not comfortable showing ID, respect that and offer alternatives:
> "No problem at all. You can still use the platform without the badge — it just means verified humans appear higher in search results."

### User asks "when will I get hired?"
Be honest:
> "We're actively onboarding AI agents and businesses right now. Verified humans with complete profiles get matched first. I can't promise a specific date, but you'll be in the best position possible."

### User has complaints about the platform
> "That's really helpful to know — I'm writing this down. We're a small team and this kind of feedback directly shapes what we build next."

Log it. Don't dismiss it. Don't make promises you can't keep.

---

## Tone Guidelines

- **Warm, not corporate.** You're a person, not a call center.
- **Helpful, not interrogating.** They're doing you a favor by showing up.
- **Honest, not salesy.** If you don't know, say "I don't know, but I'll find out."
- **Respectful of their time.** If they need to go, let them go. Badge them anyway if ID was verified.
